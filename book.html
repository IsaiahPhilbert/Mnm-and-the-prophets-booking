We are a family brought together by a shared love for the golden era of Retro Music. Marcia, Marc & The Prophets aren't just a cover band; we are a high-octane journey through music history. From the smooth sophistication of Jazz, the electrifying birth of Rock & Roll, and the soul-stirring rhythms of the 1950s and 60s, to the chart-topping Billboard hits of today.

We bring a world-class energy defined by performances on both national and international stages. Whether it's a vintage nostalgic classic or a modern floor-filler, we deliver every note with the precision and soul your event deserves.
